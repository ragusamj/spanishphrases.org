About

Spanish Phrases was created to help people learn Spanish the fastest way possible. Traditionally flash cards have been used to memorize items such as letters, numbers, and shapes.

We decided to create a digital version of the flash cards concept, where people could flip back and forth between cards to learn common phrases quickly.

This site should be used a reference for whenever someone is on the go or travelling in a country where Spanish is the dominant language.
